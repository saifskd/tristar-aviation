About Us

Tristar Aviation was founded in 2020 with an extensive portfolio of expertise and capabilities. We deliver specialized technical services that ensure aircraft assets remain airworthy, compliant, and profitable throughout their lifecycle. Our professional team of experts supports aircraft lessors, airlines, and fleet owners with precise, efficient solutions — from acquisition to lease return and beyond. Whether managing a transition, overseeing maintenance, or supporting repossession, we operate with transparency, independence, and global aviation standards. Our main priority is to protect our client’s interests, whether that be the upkeeping of contractual terms, retaining the value of your assets or ensuring that all lease terms are fully compliant with contractual obligations. Our independence from aircraft maintenance companies and manufacturers allows us to put our clients’ interests first. Integrity, diligence and commitment are what defines the foundation of our services. We provide reliable aircraft inspections, asset management, CAMO services, and end-of-life solutions with decades of industry experience, we deliver technical excellence and trusted results across the aviation lifecycle & we take pride in delivering quality, reliability, and peace of mind.
