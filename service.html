Lessors, Financiers and Banks

With years of experience, Tristar Aviation has proven itself to be the preferred partner for aviation lessors, financiers, and banks. We understand the relationship between engineering, airworthiness, and aircraft finance. We also understand how this relationship affects your aircraft’s value.

Our comprehensive array of aviation leasing technical services and CAMO solutions are directly tailored to ensure your asset retains its value. We are well established, and supporting aircraft finance and leasing is the core of what we do. Our services are designed to minimize transfer and off-lease times and their associated technical costs, which in turn maximizes the value of your aircraft and protects the sustainability and integrity of your investment.

At Tristar Aviation, we routinely manage the technical aspects of large-scale aircraft transfer projects on behalf of leading leasing companies and financiers. This service includes acceptance of the aircraft from returning lessee, off-lease CAMO, complex modification campaigns, and deliveries to new customers.

Furthermore, our asset value protection solutions are widely used to ensure that leased aircraft are well maintained and fully comply with all relevant rules and regulations. Our various asset auditing and shadow CAMO services are tailored to our clients’ exact risk management needs.

The Tristar Aviation consultant team has years of experience with technical records and aircraft maintenance, providing us with a comprehensive understanding of the engineering, airworthiness, and regulatory implications of the aircraft finance and leasing sector. Our unique combination of technical consulting expertise and CAMO qualifications makes Tristar Aviation the go-to for all your aviation leasing, financing, and banking industry services.
