Aircraft Types We Support

At Tristar Aviation, we provide expert technical and asset management services across a wide range of aircraft platforms. Our team has hands-on experience with leading Airbus, Boeing, and ATR models — ensuring safe, compliant, and cost-effective solutions for lessors, operators, and aviation partners worldwide.

Airbus A320 Family

A trusted narrow-body workhorse for short and medium-haul flights. We support all major variants, including A318, A319, A320, and A321, as well as the NEO versions (A319neo, A320neo, A321neo).

Airbus A330 Family

A versatile wide-body aircraft built for efficiency and range. We provide full lifecycle support for A330-200, A330-300, and the next-generation A330-800neo and A330-900neo.

Boeing 737 NG Family

The backbone of many airline fleets, designed for reliability and performance. We cover 737-600, 737-700, 737-800, and 737-900/900ER variants with comprehensive technical support.

Boeing 747 Family

An iconic aircraft known for long-haul and freighter operations. Our expertise spans 747-100, 747-200, 747-300, 747-400 (including freighter), and the latest 747-8/747-8F models.

Boeing 777 Family

One of the most successful wide-body aircraft in service today. We manage inspections and transitions for 777-200, 777-200ER, 777-300, 777-300ER, and the new 777X series (777-8, 777-9).

Boeing 787 Dreamliner

A modern, fuel-efficient aircraft built for global operations. We provide services for all variants: 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10.

ATR 42 & ATR 72

Reliable regional turboprops serving short-haul routes worldwide. We support ATR 42-500, ATR 42-600, ATR 72-500, and ATR 72-600 models.
